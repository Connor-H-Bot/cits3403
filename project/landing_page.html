How To Play

Click on a tweet and try to guess the Trump tweeet.

Depending on the diffculty selected you have a pre determined amount of tries to guess which of the two is the trump tweet. A counter a the bottom of the screen will appear after your first guess and display a running total of how many tweets you have selected correctly

After each guess two new randomised tweets will be display for you to chose from.

If you wish to restart your attempt please press the restart icon (Next to the settings button on the navigation bar to the top right of the page)
